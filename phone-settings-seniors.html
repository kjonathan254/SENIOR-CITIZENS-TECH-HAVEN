Make Text Bigger and Easier to Read

📱 On Android (Samsung, Tecno, Infinix, Oppo, Itel)

Step by Step

How to Increase Text Size — Android

  • Open Settings on your phone
  • Tap Display
  • Tap Font Size and Style
  • Move the slider to the right until the text feels comfortable
  • Optional: Turn on Bold Font to make letters darker and clearer

🍎 On iPhone

Step by Step

How to Increase Text Size — iPhone

  • Open Settings
  • Tap Accessibility
  • Tap Display & Text Size → Larger Text
  • Turn on Larger Accessibility Sizes
  • Drag the slider to your preferred size
✅ Why This Matters

Increasing font size takes less than one minute, requires no technical knowledge, and can make your phone feel dramatically more comfortable to use — immediately.

Turn On High Contrast for Better Visibility

Some phone screens use pale, light-coloured text that can be very hard to read — especially in bright sunlight or in a dimly lit room. High Contrast mode makes text darke[...]

📱 On Samsung

Step by Step

High Contrast — Samsung

  • Open Settings → Accessibility → Visibility Enhancements
  • Under Display Mode, select High Contrast
  • Also turn on: High Contrast Fonts and High Contrast Keyboard

📱 On Other Android Phones

Step by Step

High Contrast — Android

  • Open Settings → Accessibility
  • Look for High Contrast Text or Colour and Motion
  • Turn on High Contrast Text

🍎 On iPhone

Step by Step

High Contrast — iPhone

  • Open Settings → Accessibility → Display & Text Size
  • Turn on Increase Contrast
  • Also consider: Reduce Transparency
💡 Did You Know?

Use Your Voice Instead of Typing

Typing on a small touchscreen keyboard can be genuinely difficult — especially if you have arthritis, trembling hands, or simply find the buttons too small. You d[...]

🎙️ How to Use Voice Typing — Any Android Phone

Step by Step

Send a WhatsApp Message With Your Voice

  • Open WhatsApp and go to the chat you want to reply to
  • Tap the message box at the bottom
  • Look for the small microphone icon on your keyboard (bottom right)
  • Tap the microphone and speak slowly and clearly
  • Your words will appear as text automatically
  • Tap Send when you are done
✅ No More Struggling With Small Buttons
  • Works in WhatsApp, SMS, Notes, and email
  • Supports Kiswahili and English on most phones
  • Especially helpful for arthritis, hand tremors, or poor eyesight
  • You can also send WhatsApp Voice Notes — hold the microphone inside WhatsApp and speak

📱 Setting Up Emergency SOS — Samsung Galaxy

Step by Step

Emergency SOS on Samsung

  • Open Settings → Safety and Emergency
  • Tap Emergency contacts — add a trusted family member's number
  • Tap Medical info — add your blood type and conditions
  • Tap Emergency SOS and turn it on
  • To activate: press the power button 5 times rapidly

🍎 Setting Up Emergency SOS — iPhone

Step by Step

Emergency SOS on iPhone

  • Open Settings → Emergency SOS
  • Turn on Call with Side Button
  • Turn on Share Emergency Contacts
  • To activate: press and hold the side button and a volume button together

Simplify Your Home Screen

A cluttered screen with dozens of apps and tiny icons can feel genuinely overwhelming. A cleaner screen means less stress, fewer mistakes, and faster access to what[...]

📱 Use Easy Mode — Samsung Galaxy

Step by Step

Enable Easy Mode on Samsung

  • Open Settings → Display → Easy Mode
  • Turn it on — your home screen immediately becomes larger and simpler
  • Select which apps appear on your simplified home screen

📱 Remove Clutter — Any Android Phone

  • Long-press any unused app icon → tap Remove from Home Screen
  • Move your top 5 apps (WhatsApp, Calls, Camera, Clock, Messages) to the first screen
  • Use a simple, plain wallpaper so icons stand out clearly
  • Increase icon size: Settings → Display → Icon Size (available on Samsung)
  • Turn off notification badges for apps that are not important to you
