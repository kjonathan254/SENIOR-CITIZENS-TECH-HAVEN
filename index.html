Our Story

Built with Passion,
From Nakuru, Kenya

Senior Citizens Tech Haven was born from a simple belief — that no one should be left behind in the digital age. Founded in 2024 by Kevin Jonathan Otieno, this platform started as a blog and podcast dedicated to helping older Kenyans navigate technology with confidence.

Now in 2026, with AI tools that were barely imaginable when we started, we're rebuilding bigger — adding an AI companion, interactive learning, and connecting with SautiSahihi to serve Kenya's seniors even better.

We serve seniors directly, their families and caregivers, and institutions looking to support digital inclusion for older Kenyans.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Senior Citizens Tech Haven ("we", "our", "us") is a digital literacy platform based in Nakuru, Kenya, operated by Kevin Jonathan Otieno. Our website is located at seniorcitizenstechhaven.netlify.app. We are dedicated to helping senior citizens in Kenya navigate technology with confidence.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website:

  • Usage data such as pages visited, time spent on site, and browser type — collected automatically via Google Analytics
  • Contact information (name, email, phone number) only when you voluntarily contact us via email or phone
  • Questions or messages submitted through the Elsah AI chat feature

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as national ID numbers, financial information, or medical records.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide support
  • Understand how visitors use our platform through analytics
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data including pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/city level). This data is processed by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Our use of advertising helps us keep all content on Senior Citizens Tech Haven free of charge.

6. Elsah AI Assistant

Our Elsah AI chat feature is powered by Groq (GroqCloud). Questions you type into the chat are processed securely via our server and sent to Groq's AI service to generate responses. We do not store your chat conversations on our servers. Please do not share sensitive personal information such as your ID number, M-Pesa PIN, or bank details through the chat.

Our website contains links to external websites including Google Sites, Spotify, LinkedIn, and X (Twitter). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies when you visit them.

8. Children's Privacy

Senior Citizens Tech Haven is designed for adults aged 50 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted personal information to us, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information submitted to us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use caution when sharing any personal information online.

10. Your Rights
